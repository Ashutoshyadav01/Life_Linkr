Basic Grammer lesson

Every monday, we consistently focus on basic grammar lessons. These
sessions ensure you have a solid grasp of essential grammar rules and
sentence structures, crucial for effective communication.

Tue

Role Play & Debate Sessions

Regular role play and debate sessions are a key part of our curriculum.
These interactive activities help you practice real-life conversations and ,
articulate your thoughts clearly, boosting your speaking confidence.

Role Play & Debate Sessions

We include frequent situation speaking acts to improve your fluency and
